**Hotel Management System (HybridRooms)**
Hybritek HybridRooms is powerful, feature-rich, web-based hotel reservation software engineered to bring simplicity to your day-to-day hotel management chores. In bright colours and grid, HybridRooms presents a Reservation / Guest Management Dashboard. 
It is easy to use yet powerful and intuitive hotel reservation software which provides a clear and efficient user interface to book and manage guest/customers, hotel resources and prepare related accounts.
Its ultra-simplicity and excellent information presentation style makes this software the ideal assistance for managing guest reservations and facilities of Hotels, Motels, Hostels, Resorts, B&B, Inns, Serviced Apartments and other short-let properties.
This is a true multi-currency, multi-task software that runs all the major hotel chores from reservation, restaurant, housekeeping, maintenance to basic accounting functions.  
HybridRooms tracks and manage your property in vivid graphics & colours without losing track of the most important information. At a glance, it tells you who’s staying in what room, how much has been paid, type of customer, booking type, date and much more details – You don’t even  have to make a single click to get all these information.

**Features:**
**Room Management**
Automatically shows rooms that require make-up (Housekeeping)
Automatically shows rooms that require maintenance
Show Unconfirmed / Confirmed bookings 
Show rooms in use (in red) and Occupant details
Show room revenue profile
Automatically update Room availability 
Synchronize Web-based bookings Real-Time.
Customer Management
Automatically creates a Customer Database
Customer Booking History
View Customer Accounts including Accounts that are past due 
View Customer's Booking / Room Status
View late check-outs
Record Customer Contact and Address Details
Customer Quick Find

**Reports**
Guest Check-In Cards
Checked Out Report
Payment Reports
Billings / Revenue Reports
Room Occupancy Report – Weekly / Monthly etc
Other Reports
Export reports to other standard softwares – Excel, PeachTree, Adobe etc.

Developed using:
PHP 5+, MySQL, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, Ajax, JavaScript, Git, Apache, Restful APIs.
